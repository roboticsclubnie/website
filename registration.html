About the Event

Roborikishi is an annually held national-level event conducted by The Department of Mechanical Engineering and Robotics Club at NIE. In this event, students from various colleges participate and compete across several categories. The goal is to spread awareness about robotics and automation among students of all disciplines. Competitions include Line Follower, Maze Solver, Robo War, and Robo Race.
